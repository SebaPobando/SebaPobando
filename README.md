### 🧝‍♂️ About Me

I'm a **Full-Stack Developer** and **Technical Instructor** based in Osorno, Chile. I specialize in building scalable web applications, automating workflows, and designing RESTful APIs across the **Python** and **JavaScript** ecosystems.

Beyond shipping code, I lead technical training programs and design curriculum for enterprise clients, helping the next generation of developers reach their full potential.

I hold a **Technical Degree in Programming & Systems Analysis** and I'm currently pursuing a degree in **Computer Engineering**. I'm passionate about clean code, continuous learning, and building products that make a real impact.
